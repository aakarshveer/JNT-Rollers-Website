JNT PU Roller

Unmatched Toughness and Load-Bearing Capacity

JNT PU (Polyurethane) rollers represent the pinnacle of durability and performance in demanding industrial environments. Renowned for their exceptional toughness, they are often used as a superior substitute for traditional rubber, plastic, and even metal components.

Key Features & Benefits:

  • Extreme Abrasion & Tear Resistance: Significantly outlasts conventional rubbers in high-wear applications.
  • High Load-Bearing Capacity: Can support heavy loads and high pressure without cracking or deforming.
  • Excellent Oil & Solvent Resistance: Performs reliably in environments with oils, greases, and many chemicals.
  • Moisture Resistant: Completely unaffected by moisture and has no reaction with water.

Primary Applications:

Steel and Aluminum Processing, Woodworking and Sanding Machinery, Heavy-Duty Conveyor Systems, Coating and Printing.

Technical Specifications:

Max. Temperature Upto 100°C
Moisture Effect No reaction with water
Load Capacity Very High
Surface Finish Smooth or Textured as required
