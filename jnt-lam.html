JNT LAM Roller

Flawless Finishing for Lamination

JNT LAM rollers are precision-engineered for all types of lamination applications, where a perfect, defect-free finish is essential. These rollers are designed to apply uniform pressure and heat, ensuring a smooth, bubble-free application of protective and decorative films.

Key Features & Benefits:

  • Anti-Solvent Properties: Resists degradation from common lamination adhesives and solvents.
  • High Abrasion Strength: Built for durability and a long lifespan under constant pressure.
  • Uniform Hardness: Ensures even pressure for a bubble-free, perfect finish.
  • Excellent Surface Finish: Imparts a flawless gloss or matte finish onto the laminated product.

Primary Applications:

Post-Press Finishing (Brochures, Menus), Photo Lamination, ID Card Lamination, Flexible Packaging Lamination.

Technical Specifications:

Max. Temperature Upto 75°C
Hardness (Shore A) 70° to 75°
Solvent Resistance High
Surface Finish Smooth, Precision Ground
