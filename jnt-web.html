JNT WEB Roller

High-Speed Precision for Web Offset Printing

JNT WEB rollers are specifically engineered to meet the intense demands of modern web offset printing presses. Designed for high-speed, continuous-feed operations, these rollers ensure flawless performance, exceptional print quality, and maximum uptime.

Key Features & Benefits:

  • High-Speed Stability: Dynamically balanced to perform without vibrations at the highest press speeds.
  • Excellent Ink Transfer: Ensures uniform ink distribution for sharp, vibrant images.
  • Heat & Chemical Resistance: Withstands heat buildup and resists aggressive inks and cleaning solvents.
  • Superior Dimensional Stability: Resists swelling and shrinking to maintain precise nip pressure.

Primary Applications:

Newspaper Printing Presses, Magazine and Catalogue Production, High-Volume Book Printing, Commercial Leaflets.

Technical Specifications:

Press Compatibility All major Web Offset Presses
Hardness (Shore A) Custom-made as per requirement
Standard Color Black / Dark Gray
Surface Finish Precision Ground for optimal performance
