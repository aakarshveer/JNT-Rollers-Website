JNT FLEX Roller

Superior Performance for Flexographic Printing

JNT FLEX rollers are specially designed to excel in the dynamic environment of flexographic printing. Ideal for printing on a variety of substrates, these rollers are crucial for achieving high-quality and consistent results.

Key Features & Benefits:

  • Excellent Chemical Resistance: Formulated to handle a wide range of water-based, solvent-based, and UV flexo inks.
  • Precise Ink Transfer: Ensures consistent and accurate ink transfer from the anilox roller to the plate.
  • Durability & Long Life: Made with highly abrasion-resistant compounds for longevity in high-speed printing.
  • Constant Hardness: Maintains consistent hardness for reliable and repeatable print quality over long runs.

Primary Applications:

Flexible Packaging (pouches, bags), Label Printing, Corrugated Box Printing, Paper Cup and Tissue Printing.

Technical Specifications:

Max. Temperature Upto 55°C
Hardness (Shore A) 50° to 55°
Ink Compatibility Water-based, Solvent-based, UV
Surface Finish Precision Ground for accurate ink transfer
