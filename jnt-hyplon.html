JNT HYPLON Roller

Ultimate Resistance for Demanding Applications

JNT HYPLON rollers are engineered from high-performance Chlorosulfonated Polyethylene (CSM), a premium synthetic rubber renowned for its exceptional resistance to harsh chemicals, extreme temperatures, and environmental factors.

Key Features & Benefits:

  • Exceptional Chemical Resistance: Superior resistance to strong acids, bases, and oxidizing agents.
  • Excellent Solvent Resistance: Maintains structural integrity when exposed to a wide range of industrial solvents.
  • Great Dimensional Stability: Resists swelling and distortion, ensuring consistent performance.
  • High-Temperature Performance: Reliably operates at elevated temperatures up to 130°C.

Primary Applications:

Plastic Film Manufacturing, Chemical Processing Industries, Steel and Metal Processing, Textile Printing.

Technical Specifications:

Max. Temperature Upto 130°C
Hardness (Shore A) 60° to 92°
Chemical Resistance Excellent
Surface Finish Smooth, Precision Ground
